Hello, thank you for finding us!

Nervous Health is a dedicated resource for mental health awareness. We provide guidance, tools, and support to help individuals better understand their well-being and access the help they deserve.

About Nervous Health

Nervous Health is a school project created for Code Institute. It is a mental health awareness site that blends researched content, personal insight, and a realistic, immersive approach to support visitors who may be struggling.

Our Purpose

The goal is to provide a safe, calming space online. The landing page is designed to soothe and reassure, helping users pause and breathe before exploring further resources.

Our Resources

A dedicated library offers guides and materials tailored to different mental health issues and learning styles — from articles and videos to reflective exercises and links to external support.

Personal Connection

This project reflects some of my own experiences and perspectives, aiming to make the content feel authentic and relatable. Realism and empathy are central to its design and message.

Immediate Help

Throughout the site, clear “Call a Helpline” buttons connect users directly to UK emergency numbers. If outside the UK, a longer list of international helplines is also provided.

Nervous Health is built to inform, to calm, and to guide — one visitor at a time.
